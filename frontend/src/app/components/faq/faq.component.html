Frequently asked questions

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What products do you offer in your online store?

We specialize in providing a diverse range of fossils, meteorites, and minerals. Our collection includes rare specimens from various geological eras and locations.

Are your fossils, meteorites, and minerals authentic?

Absolutely! We take pride in offering only genuine and authenticated items. Each product is thoroughly examined by experts to ensure its authenticity.

Do you provide information about the origin of the specimens?

Yes, we strive to provide detailed information about the origin, geological significance, and age of each fossil, meteorite, and mineral. You can find this information on the product pages.

How are the items packaged for shipping?

We take great care in packaging our items to ensure their safe delivery. Fossils, meteorites, and minerals are securely wrapped and cushioned to prevent any damage during transit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal for a secure and convenient shopping experience.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide international shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on the destination. Please check our shipping information for more details.

What is your return policy?

We have a hassle-free return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 10 days of receiving it. Please review our Returns and Exchanges page for more details.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package through our website or the shipping carrier's website.

Yes, many of our items are excellent for educational purposes. Contact us for information about educational discounts or special requests for educational institutions.

For any questions or concerns, you can reach our customer support team via our Contact Us page.
